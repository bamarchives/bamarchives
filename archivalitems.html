Do I Have Archival Items?

Yes!

Nearly every department creates content with historical value but the type and amount varies. The Archives has spent time creating a comprehensive guide to assist staff with identifying important records, you'll find your department below.

Do you think you have something that isn't listed? Please contact us! [archives@bam.org]

Building Operations

Send to the Archives
Facilities Drawings (Official Copies) Original drawings relating to modifications to the buildings created for Building Operations by architects or others without involvement by Capital Projects. For physical drawings, submit once no longer needed. For pdf files, either email files to Archives, or digitally transfer on annual Records Day.
Operations Newsletters Staff wide updates about current building operations activity and decisions. Include archives email on mailing lists.
Maintain Within Your Department
Work Orders (Location Based) Building maintenance and repairs work orders not tied to a specific asset (i.e. removing trash). Remove a minimum of one year after work is completed
Preventative Maintenance Records Records of equipment or other building components that require preventative maintenance.
Key Carrier Logs Records of persons to whom physical keys were assigned on given dates.
Work Orders (Asset Based) Building maintenance and repairs work orders tied to a specific piece of equipment or other building element. Maintain permanently
Permits Place of Assembly permits, work permits, street closure permits, etc.
Policies and Procedures (Major, Official Copies) Adopted by BAM for important matters.
Service Contracts Maintenance agreements, other contracts with vendors. 7 yrs after termination
Vendor Files Information about a vendor, including copies of invoices, correspondence, etc. Remove only after a min of 5 yrs
Off-Site Storage Records Records of materials shipped to offsite storage, ordered for destruction, and payments.
Emergency Action Plan Logbook Created and maintained pursuant to 3 RCNY 6-02(m)(3)(iv).
Required Inspection Reports including elevator, boiler, and sprinkler system Inspection reports prepared by qualified BAM employees who holds any required certification.
Material Safety Data Sheets (Non-Hazardous Substances) For substances that pose no danger to human health. Remove when no longer in use.
Material Safety Data Sheets--Hazardous Substances For toxic substances as specified in 29 CFR 1910.1020(d)(1)(ii)(B). 30 years after substance is no longer present in workplace
Incident Reports Prepared by security staff for injuries, illness, lost items, other events to which staff responds. 6 yrs from date of incident or final resolution of insurance claims or litigation, whichever is longer.
Fire Safety/Emergency Action Plans Created pursuant to NYC Administrative Code, Section 27-4267 and submitted to NYC Fire Department. Remove 3 yrs after superseded
Security Guard Records Applications, registration cards, fingerprints, CPR certifications, and other records for security guards licensed by State of New York as specified in New York State Security Guard Act (General Business Law, Article 7A). Remove 1 yr after termination of employee
Security Duty Log Indicates which guards worked on a given date. Remove after 3 yrs
Security Log Books Records of visitors to the property.
Project Files Includes job contracts, permits, warranties, all documents relevant to the labor and materials involved. life cycle of product
Training Records Information indicating compliance with required safety training for employees. Retain until superceded
Grievance Records Investigative records, hearing records, appeals, correspondence, other records related to members of DC-37, AFSCME. Remove only after minimum of 15 yrs after resolution
Departmental Budgets Annual budgets and supporting documentation, including drafts and contingency budgets. Remove when no longer useful, or after 3 yrs.
Contract and Legal Agreements (Official Copies) Original signed copy of any contract or agreement entered into by this department. Remove 7 yrs after termination or final payment
